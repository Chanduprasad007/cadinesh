About the Firm

Where expertise
meets personal attention.

J Dinesh & Co is a Bengaluru-based chartered accountancy firm founded on the belief that every client — individual or corporate — deserves the same quality of financial guidance that was once available only to the largest enterprises.

Led by CA Dinesh Jagarlamudi, the firm brings deep expertise in Indian taxation law, GST compliance, corporate structuring and audit — serving salaried professionals, entrepreneurs, MSMEs and corporates.

With dual engagement as Founder of J Dinesh & Co and as a practicing CA at Essae Global, Dinesh brings a rare blend of corporate finance exposure and the personal responsiveness of a boutique practice.
