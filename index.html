About the Firm

Where expertise meets
personal attention.

J Dinesh & Co is a Bengaluru-based chartered accountancy firm founded on the belief that every client — individual or corporate — deserves the same quality of financial guidance that was once available only to the largest enterprises.

Led by CA Dinesh Jagarlamudi, the firm brings deep expertise in Indian taxation law, GST compliance, corporate structuring, and audit — serving a diverse client base spanning salaried professionals, entrepreneurs, MSMEs, and listed companies.

With dual engagement as founder of J Dinesh & Co and as a practicing CA at Essae Global, Dinesh brings a rare combination of hands-on corporate finance exposure alongside the personalized responsiveness of a boutique advisory practice.

What We Offer

Comprehensive tax &
financial services.

From individual ITR filing to complex corporate compliance — all under one trusted practice.

Income Tax Filing (ITR)
Accurate and timely ITR filing for salaried individuals, freelancers, HUFs, and business owners. We maximise your deductions within the legal framework.
Individual · Business · HUF
GST Registration & Filing
End-to-end GST services — registration, monthly/quarterly returns (GSTR-1, 3B), annual return (GSTR-9), and reconciliation with audit support.
GSTN · Returns · Audit
TDS Compliance & Filing
Correct TDS computation, challan payments, quarterly return filing (24Q, 26Q, 27Q, 27EQ), and issuance of Form 16/16A.
Quarterly Returns · Form 16
Tax Planning & Advisory
Proactive, year-round tax planning to reduce your liability legally — investment structuring, 80C/80D planning, and capital gains optimisation.
LTCG · Investments · Advisory
Corporate Tax Compliance
Full corporate tax services including company ITR, MAT computation, advance tax planning, transfer pricing, and ROC filings.
Pvt Ltd · LLP · Corporates
Startup & MSME Services
Entity incorporation (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC), MSME registration, startup India recognition, bookkeeping, payroll, and CFO advisory.
Incorporation · Payroll · CFO
Audit & Assurance
Statutory audit, tax audit (3CD), internal audit, stock audit, and bank branch audits — conducted with technical rigour and independence.
Statutory · Tax · Internal
Notice & Litigation Support
Expert representation before Income Tax Officers, CIT (Appeals), ITAT, and GST authorities. Drafting replies, submissions, and compliance letters.
ITD · ITAT · GST Authority
International Taxation
DTAA advisory, FEMA/RBI compliances, NRI tax filing, foreign remittance certificates (15CA/15CB), and cross-border structuring.
NRI · FEMA · DTAA
